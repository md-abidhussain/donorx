How DonorX Works

Connecting donors and patients in 3 simple steps.

1. Post a Request

Fill out the patient details, hospital location, and urgency level. Our system instantly publishes it to the live feed.

2. Verification

To prevent spam, our algorithms (and future moderators) flag suspicious posts. Verified requests get a blue tick.

3. Connect & Save

Nearby donors see your request. They can contact you directly via the phone number provided to arrange the donation.
